Iced Tea Sangria

Estimated Time

4 hours 15 minutes

Servings

6

INGREDIENTS

2 cups frozen DOLE® Strawberries and Blueberries Mix, partially thawed 2 cups merlot or other red wine 4 cups bottled, sweetened raspberry iced tea or your favorite fruit-flavored iced tea

Directions

Pour strawberries and blueberries into large pitcher. Pour in merlot and iced tea. Cover; refrigerate 4 hours or overnight.
