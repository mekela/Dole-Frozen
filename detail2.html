PB&J Pop

Estimated Time

2 hours 15 minutes (15 minutes active prep)

Servings

8

INGREDIENTS

2 cups frozen DOLE® Sliced Strawberries & Blueberries Mix, partially thawed ¾ cup almond milk 2 teaspoons peanut butter 8 (3 oz.) ice pop molds or paper cups

Directions

Combine berries, milk and peanut butter in blender. Cover; blend until smooth.
Pour into ice pop molds, insert stick. Freeze 2 hours or until frozen.
